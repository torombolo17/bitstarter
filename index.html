Why go biking alone when you can go with friends? Bik-e is a app made for cycists. In this awesome app you can keep in touch with your cyclists friends, you can create events and invite people to assist to your event or you can just find a route that you like and go cycling by yoursel. You can follow people and see what they have been doing, which routes do they like and what events are they going to. This app lets you keep up with the most amazing routes created by users like you. The trending area is full of the best routes and events, the ones with the best rating; oh and no wories, if you dont want to see the best routs in China, Spain, or another country that isnt yours you can allways go to the "Near Me" section to see the best routs near your location. Now all that is left to do is call tell your cyclists friends to download the app and enjoy the best routes and events for a cyclist in the world.

Bik-e lets you create custom routes so that, no matter if you are doing a workout, a route just for the fun of it or even a route for an event, you can make a route that you know you and your friends will enjoy. Creating a route is as easy as going to the map and connecting dots wherever you want to create your route. Once you hve a route that you like just hit done and like magic, your route will apear in no time, ready for you to start biking all the way.
